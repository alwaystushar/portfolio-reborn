Stefani Legal Consultants are a boutique legal consultancy focusing predominantly on Corporate and Commercial law, Financial Services and Capital Markets and Web3. At Stefani Legal Consultants, we strive on our strong reputation of providing bespoke and personal advice, allowing our clients  to achieve their objectives by advising and navigating them through the regulatory and legal framework of the UAE.

Our objective was to create a visual identity for them which reflects their vision of a premium and clean brand identity based on their values and ideology. The clean & premium serif typography was chosen to make the brand premium and clean. We used premium Black and White to convey the feel of professionalism, bold & seriousness. We also used the Royal tan color to make the brand more alive & give the sense of harmony & easiness.

From visual identity to UX/UI design, we provide all the essential elements of a brand within time & budget.
